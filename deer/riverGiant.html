172" River Bottom Giant Falls In The River

On this episode of Midwest Whitetail, we follow along with Mike Reed as he closes the chapter on yet another River Farm legend. Going back all the way to 2020, this buck shined as a beautiful main frame 8 with true potential to blow into a monarch. Fast forward all these years later and Mike's plan to hunt the ghostly buck finally came to fruition. Join Mike as he dives into his step by step process for hunting "DK" during the 2023 season dating back to finding the shed antler after a controlled burn in the spring. This one has it all from bucks chasing, buck vocalizations, 10 yard shots, and another recovery that saw Mike take to the river!
