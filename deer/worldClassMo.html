216” World Class Missouri Whitetail

In this episode of Deer Season ‘23, we take you through the full story of Terry Drury’s biggest deer to date. When the trail camera pictures started rolling in this summer, Terry knew Mick Dundee was something special. Come fall, he frequented one little clover patch and a pond tucked into the timber, so they sat one blind over and over and over trying to get a crack at him. After six encounters, he finally put on the show of all shows. DeerCast said Good, he read the script, and offered up a 21-yard shot that's as pretty as it gets!
